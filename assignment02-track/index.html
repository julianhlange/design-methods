VOCAL TRANSACTIONS


JULIAN LANGE
PSAM 5170 Assignment 02: Track
September 12-18, 2017


Almost all purchase receipts include the
name or ID of the cashier or server.
Given the often purely transactional
nature of these interactions, I scan my
receipts for this bit of information.

Over 7 days I collected the receipt and
recorded a phrase from each transaction.
They ranged from interesting to banal and
from humorous to jarring.

Here they are in poster and mobile form.
